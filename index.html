Security Standards

Length Over Variety

A 16-character password of just letters is often stronger than an 8-character complex one.

No Repetition

Site leaks are common. If you use the same password everywhere, one leak ruins everything.

Use Mnemonics

A string like "BigRedCat@2022" is easy to remember but incredibly hard for bots to guess.

MFA is Essential

Always enable Multi-Factor Authentication. It's your second line of defense.
